Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026.

What we collect

We collect minimal information: anonymized page-view analytics, basic referrer data, and — if you leave a comment or contact us — the email address you provide. We do not sell your data.

Cookies and tracking

We use privacy-respecting analytics to understand which articles are read. Affiliate links on this site may set cookies from our partner merchants to track referrals; we do not read or store the content of those cookies ourselves.

Third parties

When you click an affiliate link, you leave this site and land on a merchant's page (for example, reprievehealth.com). That merchant's privacy policy governs what happens with your data after that click.

Your rights

If you are in the EU, UK, California, or another jurisdiction with data rights legislation (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. Email us at the contact page.
